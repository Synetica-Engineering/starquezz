Rocket journey
A trip toward the anchor event — each week is one hop closer to the destination (the camp-out). Excellent for event-dated dreams.
A path implies a finish line / “race” — guard against it feeling like falling behind.

My read

Constellation → galaxy is the natural winner: the app is literally StarqueZZ, so the long-term container being made of stars unifies daily / weekly / lifetime into one language — small star, big star, picture, galaxy. The forming image carries the anticipation.
Brick stays the strongest runner-up for its sheer tactility — great for younger kids who love building.
These aren’t exclusive: the galaxy could be the all-time “every dream we’ve ever chased” wall, while each active dream uses a constellation (or a brick/rocket the family picks). Container could even be a per-dream choice in setup.
Whatever we pick: the weekly token landing must be a distinct, weightier beat than a daily star — earned in the Sunday ceremony, never mid-week.
